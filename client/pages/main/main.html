Sheffield Info

Sheffield is located in South Yorkshire, the center of England. It now has been the fourth largest city in United Kingdom. Sheffield is home to a variety of people from different cultural backgrounds, you can find at least 84 kinds of languages in this city. Sheffield was an industrial city, famous for iron and steel manufacturing. Certain innovation of industrial aspects, such as, crucible and stainless steel, promoted the industrial development of the city. In recent years, with the increasing prestige of Sheffield United Football Club, Sheffield has become more diversified and active.


University of Sheffield

The University of Sheffield, situated in Sheffield, is one of the world's hundred elite schools with a good reputation in the UK. Dating back to 1828, since the University was found, it has trained a total of five Nobel laureates. The university contains five academic faculties with multiple departments. Until 2015, there were 18,975 undergraduate and 8,220 postgraduate students in the University of Sheffield.


Sheffield Hallam University

Sheffield Hallam University, acronym as SHU，founded in 1843, was initially the Sheffield School of Design, after industrial revolution, it has be renamed as Sheffield City Polytechnic. After two times’s institutions merge and adjust over two centuries, the University has developed into the UK's sixth largest comprehensive university with 31,530 students (including 4,400 international students), 4,494 staff and 708 courses.
